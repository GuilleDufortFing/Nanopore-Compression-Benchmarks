# Article Results

This subdirectory is reserved for curated, versioned runs that are meant to be cited or reused in article analysis.

Runs are not generated here by default. They are first written to `results/generated/<benchmark-type>/` and then manually copied to `results/article/<benchmark-type>/` once they become part of the curated set.

The expected active subdirectories are:

- `compression/`
- `speed/`
- `time_split/`
